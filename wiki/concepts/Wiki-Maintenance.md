# Concept: Wiki Maintenance

## Overview
The process of keeping a knowledge base useful, accurate, and interlinked using LLMs.

## Challenges
- **Human Burden:** Humans often abandon wikis because the "bookkeeping" (updating links, summaries, consistency) grows too fast.
- **LLM Solution:** LLMs are ideal for repetitive maintenance tasks like cross-referencing and linting.

## Operations
- **Ingest:** Updating existing pages and creating new ones when new data arrives.
- **Lint:** Periodically checking for contradictions, stale claims, and orphan pages.
